Four.meme AI Sprint is a global online hackathon focused on real-world AI innovation — spanning AI tools, generative applications, AI agents, and integrations across Web3 and internet culture.

This 4-week sprint is designed for builders who want to experiment rapidly and ship meaningful AI products. Projects are not required to issue tokens or deploy on-chain, making the hackathon accessible to early-stage teams, solo developers, and AI enthusiasts.

Participants will gain exposure to leading AI and Web3 ecosystems, access mentorship and technical resources, and compete for a $50,000 prize pool plus incubation opportunities.

Eligibility

Open to global participants
Team size: 1–5 members
Projects must involve AI technology
AI x Web3 / AI x Meme integrations are encouraged but not mandatory
Working demo required (prototype, repo, or demo video)
Token issuance is optional and will not influence judging outcomes
Projects must comply with open-source licensing and IP requirements
Participation is free
Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated through expert review (70%) and community voting (30%).

Expert Evaluation (70%)

Innovation — originality and depth of AI application (30%)
Technical Implementation — code quality and demo stability (30%)
Practical Value — user impact or commercial potential (20%)
Presentation — clarity of pitch and execution capability (20%)
Community Voting (30%)

Top projects will be showcased publicly for community voting with anti-bot mechanisms.
