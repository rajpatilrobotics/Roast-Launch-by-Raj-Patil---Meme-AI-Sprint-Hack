# 🎬 RECORDING TIPS

1. **Pre-load** a roast in another tab as backup in case the API is slow.
2. **Talk 10% slower** than feels natural — judges watch at 1.5x.
3. **Use a real mic.** Record voice separately and sync in post.
4. **Cursor highlighter** (Mouseposé / mouse highlight) so judges follow your clicks.
5. **Soft synthwave background music at -25dB.**
6. **Hard-refresh before recording** so the trending data is fresh.
7. **Practice the cold open 5 times** — that's the only line judges judge you on.
