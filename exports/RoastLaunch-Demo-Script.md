## 🎤 Quick voice-over tips

- **Pace**: ~150 words per minute. The script above is timed for that.
  If you read fast, you'll come in at 3:30 — fine, leaves room for
  pauses.
- **Pause** before saying numbers (62 → 88, etc.) — let the visual
  land.
- **Don't read the screen.** Let people see and hear different things.
- **End on the WAGMI confetti.** Last frame should be green + smiling.
- **Energy**: this app is funny. Sound a little hyped, not corporate.

## 🎬 Recording checklist

- [ ] Two browsers ready, both logged in with different usernames
- [ ] Sound enabled in your screen recorder (system audio)
- [ ] Mute toggle in app is OFF (you want the verdict sounds + voice)
- [ ] Pre-roast one or two coins so today's count > 0 in the hero badge

      so the Coach demo lands
