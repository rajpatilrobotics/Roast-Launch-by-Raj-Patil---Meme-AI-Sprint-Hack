## 🗺️ The 7-Step User Journey (follow this in the demo)

### Step 0 — Landing
- Open the app. Pick a username (no password — instant).
- The home page hero shows: **"97% of meme coins die in 24 hours. Don't be one of them."**
- Live trending feed at the bottom shows real coins from Four.meme right now.

---

### Step 1 — 🎤 Pitch Your Coin
- A big orange input box: "Pitch your meme coin in one line."
- Either type your own (e.g. *"a frog that trades crypto in its sleep"*) or
  click one of the **rotating preset chips** (frogs, AI agents, brainrot, etc.).
- Optionally add a **token name** + **ticker** (3–5 chars).
- Hit the giant **"🔥 Roast My Coin"** button.

**Demo tip:** Use a deliberately weak idea like *"another dog coin"* so the
judges roast it hard — the comeback later is more dramatic.

---

### Step 2 — 🎤 Meet the AI Judges
A 3-second countdown plays, then three AI personas appear side-by-side:

| Judge | Role | Color |
|---|---|---|
| 🐂 **The Bull** | Degen optimist — finds the bullish angle | Green |
| 🧐 **The Skeptic** | Crypto-Twitter analyst — pokes holes in the pitch | Blue |
| 🚨 **Rug Detector** | On-chain analyst — flags rug patterns | Red |

Each judge writes a 50–80 word roast in their own voice with specific
references to your pitch. **Click "🎙️ Hear the roast"** to have the AI read
it out loud.

---

### Step 3 — 📊 The Numbers (Verdict Reveal)
A massive **0–100 Survive Score™** counts up dramatically with confetti.

Verdicts:
- 🎉 **WAGMI** (70+) — "Your coin might actually make it"
- ⚠️ **DYOR** (40–69) — "Needs work but has potential"
- 💀 **NGMI** (<40) — "Dead on arrival"

Below the score, a clean breakdown card shows:
- **4 sub-scores** (Narrative, Community, Timing, Risk — each /25)
- **☠️ Rug Probability** bar (0–100%)
- **🎓 Graduation Probability** bar — odds your coin hits Four.meme's $80K mcap
  threshold and migrates to PancakeSwap (with the AI's reasoning)
- **Meta-fit pill** — does your idea match what's trending right now?

---

### Step 4 — 🛠️ Fix It Before You Launch (the killer feature)
The AI lists **specific, concrete fixes** to improve your idea — not generic
advice, but things like *"Tie the gimmick to the Italian brainrot meta — use
'Tralalero' in the name"*.

Below the fixes is a **7-day launch playbook** (Day 1 → Day 7 actions).

🟣 **AI COACH BUTTON — show this off:**
Click **"✨ Apply fixes & re-roast"** — the AI rewrites your pitch with all
its own suggestions baked in, then re-scores it. A purple comparison panel
appears showing:
- **Score: 32 → 71** (+39 ✓)
- **Rug %: 78 → 41** (−37 ✓)
- **Graduation %: 18 → 62** (+44 ✓)
- Side-by-side verdict change (NGMI → WAGMI)

Click **"✅ Use the improved version"** to swap in the upgraded coin
everywhere on the page. **Confetti pops.** Judges will love this moment.

---

### Step 5 — 🚀 Launch on Four.meme (the climax)
The launch panel shows a **5-point pre-flight checklist**:
- ✅ Score ≥ 70
- ✅ Token name set
- ✅ Valid ticker (3–5 chars)
- ✅ At least 2 meme captions
- ⚠️ Fits current meta (warning, not blocker)

**Two paths, both clickable:**
- All checks pass → **glowing green pulsing CTA**
  *"🚀 Launch on Four.meme — Ready to ship!"*
- Some fail → orange **"🚧 Launch anyway"** button
  *(failed checks show as recommendations underneath, not blockers)*

Either path opens **Four.meme with the token name, ticker, and description
pre-filled via URL params**. There's also a **"Copy launch payload"** button
for manual paste.

When you launch, it's logged to your activity history with type `launch` 🚀.

---

### Step 6 — 🎨 Make It Shareable
Two assets are auto-generated:

**A. The Shareable Roast Card** (centered, downloadable)
- Big branded card with score + verdict + bull/rug snippets
- Buttons: **⬇ Download Card** (PNG) | **𝕏 Share on X**

**B. Meme Caption Cards** (full-width row, 3 cards)
- AI-generated meme captions for your token
- Each card has 3 buttons: **⬇ PNG** · **📋 Copy** (copies image to clipboard
  for instant pasting into Telegram/Discord) · **𝕏 Share**

Below: **Voting** — let the community thumbs-up or thumbs-down your roast.

---

### Step 7 — ⛓ Prove It On-Chain (subtle, optional)
Compact one-liner: *"Permanently anchor this roast to BNB Chain."*

Click **💾 Save on-chain** → simulates a BNB Chain transaction with a
realistic broadcasting animation, then shows a transaction receipt with a
hash. Useful for "this roast existed at this moment" provenance.

---

### 🌶️ Below the result: Live on Four.meme (Trending Feed)
A live grid pulled from Four.meme's API showing what's hot RIGHT NOW.
Each trending coin has a **"Roast this coin →"** button that one-clicks
the data into the input and roasts it. Great way to demo on real coins.

---

### 🔥 Recent Roasts (Activity Feed)
A list of the last 10 roasts — yours and the community's — with score and
verdict. Clickable for context.

---

## 📑 Other Tabs (Top Nav)

### 🏠 Home
The full pipeline above (Steps 1–7).

### 📜 History
Your personal activity log + community feed. Two tabs:
- **Roasts** — every coin you've roasted with score + verdict + share links
- **🚀 Launches** — coins that actually got launched (yours + top launchers
  on the leaderboard, plus a global recent-launches feed)

### ⚔️ Battles (if used in demo)
Pit two coin ideas head-to-head. Both get roasted, the higher score wins.

### 🎯 Right-side Jump Nav (only on result pages, large screens)
A floating pill bar pinned to the right edge of result pages with one-tap
jumps to: 🎤 Judges · 📊 Numbers · 🛠️ Fix · 🚀 Launch · 🎨 Share · ⛓ On-chain.
The active section auto-highlights as you scroll.
